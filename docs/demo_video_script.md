Three-Minute Demo Script
========================

Opening 30 seconds
------------------
I built Nova Cold Chain Guardian to solve a real operations gap in rural vaccine delivery. Cold chain failures are often detected too late because evidence is scattered across sensors, technician calls, and inspection records.

Problem 30 seconds
------------------
When teams triage manually, they lose time and may miss early warning patterns. The result is wasted doses, delayed immunization, and preventable risk for communities.

Solution 60 seconds
-------------------
I will show how my agentic system receives one incident payload and runs six coordinated agents. The planner defines the investigation path, sensor analyst evaluates thermal risk, visual auditor reviews inspection evidence, voice triage extracts urgency from technician speech, policy checker computes transparent severity, and action executor outputs response steps and automation tasks.

Live walkthrough 45 seconds
---------------------------
I will call the simulate endpoint, show the structured report, highlight the risk score, root causes, and recommended actions, then show how the automation plan can feed downstream operations tools.

Impact 15 seconds
-----------------
This system helps teams intervene earlier, reduce vaccine spoilage, and protect last-mile immunization outcomes in resource-constrained settings.
