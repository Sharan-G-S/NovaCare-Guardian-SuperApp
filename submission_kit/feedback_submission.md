Feedback Submission Draft
=========================

Overall Experience
------------------
I had a strong experience building with Amazon Nova capabilities, especially for multimodal interpretation and structured reasoning within agent workflows.

What Worked Well
----------------
- Nova model responses were effective for extracting operational signal from noisy field evidence.
- The architecture was flexible enough to combine deterministic policy logic and model reasoning.
- I was able to build a realistic decision pipeline rather than a single prompt chain.

Challenges I Faced
------------------
- Prompt consistency across diverse incident formats required repeated tuning.
- Demonstrating confidence and uncertainty in a user-friendly way took additional design work.
- Building a concise three-minute story while preserving technical depth was difficult.

Suggestions for Improvement
---------------------------
- I would benefit from more official examples for conflict-aware multimodal scoring patterns.
- Additional reference architectures for production-grade agent observability would help.
- More sample datasets across critical infrastructure domains would accelerate prototyping.

Potential Impact
----------------
The platform enables high-impact operational systems where fast decisions matter. In my use case, it can reduce biologic dose spoilage and improve escalation quality in vulnerable regions.
