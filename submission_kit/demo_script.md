Three Minute Demo Script
========================

Section 1 Opening 25 seconds
----------------------------
I built Nova Cold Chain Guardian to solve a high-stakes failure mode in public health logistics: biologic dose spoilage in flood-prone districts. Teams often receive fragmented evidence and must decide quickly with incomplete context.

Section 2 Problem 30 seconds
----------------------------
Field operations receive sensor exceptions, technician voice updates, and visual inspection notes in separate channels. Manual triage slows response and increases avoidable spoilage risk for antivenom and rabies post-exposure vaccines.

Section 3 Solution 65 seconds
-----------------------------
I process one incident through six specialist agents.
- Planner agent frames the investigation route.
- Sensor analyst computes thermal breach risk.
- Visual auditor interprets physical integrity warnings.
- Voice triage extracts urgency and fault clues.
- Policy checker computes severity with transparent scoring.
- Action executor outputs response actions and automation steps.

Section 4 Live Walkthrough 55 seconds
-------------------------------------
I trigger the simulation endpoint and display the final report. I show severity, risk score, and root causes, then open traces to prove how each agent contributed to the final decision. I close by showing the automation plan that can be sent to downstream operations tools.

Section 5 Impact and Close 25 seconds
-------------------------------------
This solution is designed to reduce time to escalation and prevent avoidable dose loss in high-risk communities. I built it as a practical, auditable system that can move from pilot to production.
