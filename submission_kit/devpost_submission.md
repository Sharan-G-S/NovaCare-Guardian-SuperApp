Short Description
-----------------
I built Nova Cold Chain Guardian to prevent biologic cold-chain failures for antivenom and rabies post-exposure vaccines in flood-prone districts. My system fuses telemetry, technician voice updates, and inspection evidence through a coordinated multi-agent pipeline, then outputs risk severity, root causes, and executable response actions.

How I Used Amazon Nova
----------------------
I used Amazon Nova reasoning and multimodal capabilities across the workflow. Planner, visual, and voice agents use Nova intelligence to interpret ambiguous field evidence, while policy logic applies transparent deterministic controls for final severity and escalation confidence.

Problem and Why It Matters
--------------------------
In high-risk regions, cold-chain incidents are detected too late because evidence is fragmented across systems and teams. Every delayed decision can mean lost life-saving doses. I focused on this gap because it creates direct public health consequences.

Technical Highlights
--------------------
- Multi-agent orchestration with planner, sensor analyst, visual auditor, voice triage, policy checker, and action executor.
- Multimodal evidence fusion with explainable traces.
- Policy boundary scoring for clear, auditable outcomes.
- Automation handoff plan for operational systems.
- Reliability tests for edge cases and severity thresholds.

Impact
------
I designed this to reduce mean time to triage, lower avoidable spoilage, and improve escalation quality for district health teams. The model is deployment-ready for pilot operations with phased adoption.
